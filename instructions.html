Instructions

Dear participant , , thank you for taking part in our experiment to compare a new technique in copying and pasting against the traditional copy-paste technique.

Please check you have a participant ID before you start the experiment. If you do not have one, please alert your investigator immediately.

In the next screen after you read the instructions, you will be shown the experimental interface to do a trial run (not part of the real experiment). The interface has two sections.

In the left section, there will be some text you are tasked to copy. On the right section, you will be shown a few windows with text. There will be a blank window for you to paste your text.

There will be two main techniques you are required to use: AutoComPaste and the traditional Copy-Paste technique.

How to use AutoComPast

Just start typing the text that you want to copy (the text to copy will be shown to you), and select the correct text from the dropdown list. Use the UP/DOWN arrow keys to select the text you want to copy and press ENTER or click on the option. When you are satisfied with your copied text, press the ‘Next Task’ button.

Traditional Copy-Paste Technique

Highlight the text you wish to copy that corresponds to the text on the left, and use CMD/CTRL-C to copy the text. Use CMD/CTRL-V to paste the text.

The Experiment

You will have a 1-minute break when you have completed a certain number of tasks (the system will disable the ‘Next Task’ button during that time.) Please resume your tasks after 1 minute.

After The Experiment

Please fill up the post-questionnaire for your experiences on the experiment and as part of the data collection.

You may click ‘Continue’ after you are done reading and checking the instructions to do the trial run.
